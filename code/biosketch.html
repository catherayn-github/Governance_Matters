Leon Gregory M. Batiao

Born on December 27, 2002, in Calamba City, Laguna, Leon Gregory M. Batiao is a dedicated individual with a remarkable track record of academic and professional success. He is the fourth of five siblings and has a twin, excelling from an early age by graduating with distinction from Post Elementary School.

Gregory continued his success at Camp Vicente Lim Integrated School, earning a TESDA NCIII Bookkeeping certification. Pursuing his passion for finance, he is enrolled in BS Accountancy at Batangas State University, consistently making the Dean’s List and excelling in accountancy qualifying exams.

To gain practical experience, he completed a 400-hour internship at I-Serve Business Process Incorporation, where he honed skills in bookkeeping, tax filing, financial analysis, and auditing. Gregory is proficient in accounting systems, Microsoft Office, and adheres to high ethical standards, making him a capable and resourceful professional committed to lifelong learning and excellence in the field of finance and accounting.

Mary Antonette N. Mulingtapang

Mary Antonette N. Mulingtapang is a driven and accomplished student, known for her dedication to academics and her ability to guide others. She is the eldest of two children born to Mr. Rodante B. Mulingtapang and Mrs. Baby Rune Lee N. Mulingtapang. Her journey in education began at Blessed Christ Child Montessori Foundation in Balayan, Batangas, where she completed her elementary education.

Continuing her academic pursuit, Mary Antonette attended Dacanlao Gregorio Agoncillo National High School in Calaca, Batangas, for her junior high school education, graduating with high honors in 2019. She pursued her senior high school studies at the University of Batangas and Calaca Senior High School, where she maintained her impressive academic performance and graduated with high honors in 2021.

Currently, Mary Antonette is enrolled at Batangas State University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy. Since starting her tertiary education in 2021, she has consistently demonstrated her academic prowess as a Dean’s Lister (2021–2023).

Mary Antonette is passionate about accounting and finance, aiming to excel in her chosen field through dedication and continuous learning. Her commitment to excellence and her strong educational foundation are testaments to her determination to achieve her goals.

Aileen Mae A. Pabiania

Born on May 12, 2003, in Lucena City, Aileen Mae A. Pabiania is the youngest of two children. She completed her elementary education at St. Joseph Academy of Sariaya, graduating as third honorable mention, and earned High Honors and various service awards in junior high school. In senior high school, she graduated with high honors from Maryhill College.

Aileen pursued a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy at Batangas State University – The National Engineering University, consistently earning spots on the Dean’s List. In her third year, she passed the PICAT Certified Bookkeeper exam and placed 4th in NFJPIA Region IV’s Internal Audit Training Program 4.0, gaining practical experience at Punongbayan & Araullo (P&A Grant Thornton).

She also completed a 400-hour assurance internship at Sycip Gorres Velayo & Co. (SGV & Co.), enhancing her skills in auditing, financial analysis, and client management. Currently, Aileen is a member of NFJPIA Region IV’s Internal Audit Division, applying her auditing and leadership skills, and continues her pursuit of growth and excellence in the accounting profession.
